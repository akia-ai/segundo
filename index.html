WELCOME TO

Hotel Segundo

Since 1924, Hotel Segundo has been San Diego's most distinguished address. Our historic building, lovingly restored to its original grandeur, offers guests an escape into a world of refined elegance.

Every detail has been carefully curated to create an atmosphere of understated luxury, from our hand-selected artworks to our bespoke furnishings crafted by local artisans.
